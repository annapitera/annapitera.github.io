About

My name is Anna Pitera, and I'm currently doing a survey on intrusive advertising techniques as part of my freshman research project at the Marine Academy of Technology and Environmental Science (MATES). My project is entitled "The Effect of Intrusive Advertising Techniques on Brand Image". Brand image is the customer's perception of a brand or company. If you're interested in being a part of the experiment, please read the following information:

  • You will be reading an article with various advertisements "popping up" throughout.
  • To close an advertisement, click the "Close" button on top of the image shown.
  • Please allocate a few minutes of no distractions to participate in this experiment.
  • If you want to contact me for any reason, click the "Contact" tab at the top of the web page.
  • Thank you for your interest. It is greatly appreciated!
