Diet

They normally feed on fish, crustaceans, marine worms, and mollusks. They may also consume small amounts of plants, like algae. An important fact to note is that gender and age can greatly affect their diets; males and juvenile females tend to have less diversity in their diets. Adult females will often feed on more hard-shelled mollusks and crabs than other terrapins because of their powerful jaw. While in captivity, they particularly enjoy relish lettuce, watercress, and fruit. While in the wild, they enjoy saltmarsh periwinkle.
